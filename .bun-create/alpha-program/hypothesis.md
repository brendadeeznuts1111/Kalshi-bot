# Hypothesis — no code until all five are answered

> A program that cannot state who loses the edge it captures does not get a shadow phase.

## 1. What is the edge, in one sentence?

_One sentence._

## 2. Who is on the other side, and why are they willingly losing it to me?

_If the answer is "the market is wrong," you don't have a hypothesis yet — you have a disagreement._
_"Fans, not quants" is valid; it tells you the edge decays when sharp money arrives._

## 3. Why does this edge persist?

_Capacity limits, attention, regulation, and speed are valid. "Nobody noticed" usually isn't._

## 4. What observation would falsify it?

_Pre-commit — feeds program.json gates (killBrierDriftPct for Brier sanity; graduationMinRealizedEdgeCentsPerFill for pilot)._

## 5. What's the capacity?

_At what size does your own flow move the price against you?_
